Biology Reviewer

Biology is the study of life and living organisms, from molecules to ecosystems.

  • Cell Biology: Prokaryotic & eukaryotic cells, organelles.
  • Genetics: DNA, RNA, protein synthesis, biotechnology.
  • Metabolism & Energy: ATP, respiration, photosynthesis.
  • Ecology: Biodiversity, conservation, ecosystems.
  • Evolution: Natural selection, adaptation, speciation.

Chemistry Reviewer

Chemistry is the study of matter, its properties, and the changes it undergoes.

  • Atomic Theory: Structure of atoms, isotopes.
  • Chemical Bonding: Ionic, covalent, metallic bonds.
  • Stoichiometry: Balancing equations, mole concept.
  • Thermochemistry: Energy changes, calorimetry.
  • Organic Chemistry: Hydrocarbons, polymers.

Physics Reviewer

Physics is the study of matter, energy, and the fundamental laws of nature.

  • Mechanics: Motion, forces, Newton’s laws.
  • Waves & Sound: Oscillations, acoustics.
  • Electricity & Magnetism: Circuits, electromagnetism.
  • Thermodynamics: Heat, entropy, energy transfer.
  • Modern Physics: Quantum mechanics, relativity.

Earth Science Reviewer

Earth Science studies Earth’s structure, processes, and systems.

  • Geology: Rocks, minerals, plate tectonics.
  • Meteorology: Weather, climate change.
  • Oceanography: Currents, marine ecosystems.
  • Astronomy: Earth’s place in the universe.
  • Environmental Science: Sustainability, resources.
