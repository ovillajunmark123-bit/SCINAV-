Interactive Experiments 🧪

Try these simple experiments and mark them as completed to earn rewards!

Biology: Photosynthesis in Leaves 🌱

Observe how leaves produce starch when exposed to sunlight.

  1. Take a green leaf and boil it in water for 2 minutes.
  2. Place the leaf in alcohol and heat until chlorophyll is removed.
  3. Rinse the leaf in warm water.
  4. Add iodine solution — blue-black color shows starch.

Physics: Balloon Rocket 🚀

Demonstrate Newton’s Third Law using a balloon.

  1. Thread a straw onto a string and tape the string across a room.
  2. Blow up a balloon and tape it to the straw.
  3. Release the balloon — it rockets forward!

Chemistry: Vinegar & Baking Soda Volcano 🌋

Observe an acid-base reaction producing carbon dioxide.

  1. Place baking soda inside a small container.
  2. Add vinegar slowly.
  3. Watch the fizzing reaction as CO₂ gas is released.

Earth Science: Water Filtration 🌍

Learn how soil and sand filter dirty water.

  1. Fill a plastic bottle with layers of gravel, sand, and charcoal.
  2. Pour dirty water through the filter.
  3. Observe cleaner water dripping out.
